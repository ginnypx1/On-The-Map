On the Map

"On the Map" allows Udacity students around the globe to pin their location and share a website.

"On The Map" is an iOS application developed as part of the Udacity iOS Development Nanodegree program.

It uses MapKit, URLSession networking, JSON Serialization and Reachability in order to display the locations of Udacity students currently studying around the globe.

Screenshots are provided by App Launchpad.

To download "MemeMe2.0", visit my Github repository at: On the Map.
